Got Questions?
Frequently asked
questions
How do students fill their details?
We set up a simple web form (no app download needed). Students open it on their mobile, type their name and details, take a live photo with their camera, and submit. Takes about 2 minutes per student.
What if a student makes a spelling mistake?
Our system has a self-verification step — students review and confirm their own details before submitting. Admin also approves before printing. This makes name errors nearly zero.
Do you deliver outside Coimbatore?
Yes — we deliver anywhere in Tamil Nadu. For other states, delivery may take 2 extra days. Courier charges may apply for very small orders outside Tamil Nadu.
What is the minimum order quantity?
Basic: 200 cards minimum. Standard and Premium: 100 cards minimum. For events or corporate: 50 cards minimum (pricing adjusted).
Can we add new students later in the year?
Yes — with our Standard and Premium packages, you can add new students anytime and order a small re-print batch. No need to redo the entire process.
I already have a printer. Can I just buy the software system?
Yes — our ₹3,500 Setup Only plan gives you the complete digital system deployed for your school. You handle printing using your existing setup. We provide training and support.
How do I pay?
UPI / bank transfer for setup fees. For bulk card orders, 50% advance and 50% on delivery. We also accept Razorpay payment link. No cash handling required.
